# **KeenCombat**

![](https://i.imgur.com/OqLMeEJ.jpeg)

## Adds unique weapon skills to every martial weapon type in Valheim (magic coming soooon)!

## Moves the Heavy Attack from its default button to a press and hold of the normal attack (like an action combat game should). This frees up the old Heavy Attack button for these unique skills!

## Press Middle Mouse Button (or Right Trigger on controller) to activate your equipped weapon's unique skill!

---

***NOTE: versions 0.1.2+ properly link the audio and icon files when automatically installing with R2Modman****

**Who is this mod for?**

Those looking to put a bit more action-combat into their Valheim experience. Mods like Jewelcrafting and EpicLoot are awesome, but what they end up doing (at least imo) is having you spend more time preparing for combat, than actually BEING in combat. This mod is designed for players looking to add a new, unique take on Valheim's combat that leads to a pretty dynamic experience. Spend more time playing and less time preparing!

---

### Weapon Skills

⚔ **Sword — Blink Strike Dash** forward in the direction the camera is facing, striking all enemies in your path.

🔨 **Mace — Bulwark** Flex and fortify yourself, reducing incoming damage and regenerating health for a short time.

🪓 **Axe / Dual Axe — Frenzy** Let out a battle roar (A cry many of you Vikings have heard before), temporarily boosting your attack speed and movement speed.

🔱** Atgeir / Spear — Falcon Blitz** Unleash a rapid six-hit flurry, locking yourself in place while striking everything in range.

🏹 **Crossbow — Rapid Fire** Fire six bolts in quick succession in the direction you are aiming, consuming no ammunition.

🗡 **Knife — Assassination** Vanish into the shadows with a familiar sound (to some of you). Enemies lose track of you. Press the skill button again to unleash a devastating backstrike.

⚔⚔ **2H Sword / 2H Axe — Whirlwind** Spin twice in a devastating double sweep, carving through all enemies around you while moving forward.

🏹 **Bow — Primal Rally** Call a wild beast to fight by your side. The creature summoned depends on your bow. The beast follows you, attacks your enemies, and despawns after one minute. ### Additional Features

**Auto Shield Equip** — Automatically equips your best shield when drawing a one-handed weapon. Using a config manager you can enable or disable this feature. You can also choose to enable or disable it for axes or knives.

**Skill Compendium** — All skills are documented in the in-game compendium under "Keen Combat Skills". Fully Configurable — All cooldowns and damage values are adjustable via the Configuration Manager (F1 in-game).
